ABOUT US

Trinity Computer Institute, established in the year 2015 is a software training institute located at the very mid town of Namchi South Sikkim providing its service in the field of computer education and software applications.

Trinity Computer Institute is affiliated to SARVA EDUCATION which is India No.1, Genuine, Trusted, Reputed, Top Level National Autonomous Organization for I.T, HRD, Skill Advancement Literacy programmes. It has been offered prestigious International ISO 9001:2015 Certification for its Quality Management System

In Today's Digital World where computer literacy has undoubtly become vital and invaluable for everyone our well structured job oriented courses and experienced faculties will help you to improve your Soft Skills and make you job ready for any I.T. profession jobs both in government and private sectors .
